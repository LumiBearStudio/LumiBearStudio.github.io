# Curate photos with Quick Look and the Shelf

Browse a large photo folder, instantly preview each image full-size with Quick Look, and collect the keepers on the Shelf — all without leaving the keyboard.

**Time:** ~5 minutes to learn the flow  
**Skill level:** Beginner

---

## The scenario

You've imported 200 photos from a camera. You want to pick the best ones and move them to a `Keepers` folder. Without SPAN Finder this means opening each photo in a viewer, alt-tabbing back, moving the file, repeat. Here's a faster way.

---

## Step-by-step

### 1. Navigate to your photo folder

Open the folder in SPAN Finder (Miller Columns or any view mode works).

### 2. Switch to Icon view for a visual overview

Press <kbd>Ctrl</kbd> + <kbd>4</kbd> to switch to Icon view. Press it again to cycle through size options — use **Large** or **Extra Large** for thumbnail-size previews that let you quickly eliminate obvious rejects.

### 3. Open Quick Look for a full-size preview

Click any photo to select it, then press <kbd>Space</kbd>. Quick Look opens a large preview window — roughly 70% of your screen — with the photo rendered at full resolution.

### 4. Navigate through photos without closing Quick Look

While Quick Look is open:
- Press <kbd>→</kbd> or <kbd>Space</kbd> — next photo
- Press <kbd>←</kbd> — previous photo
- Press <kbd>Esc</kbd> — close Quick Look

You can browse through hundreds of photos this way in minutes.

### 5. Add keepers to the Shelf

When you see a photo you want to keep:

1. Press <kbd>Esc</kbd> to close Quick Look (the photo stays selected)
2. Drag the selected photo to the Shelf on the right edge, or right-click → **Add to Shelf**
3. Press <kbd>Space</kbd> to open Quick Look again on the next photo

**Tip:** You can also select multiple photos at once (<kbd>Ctrl</kbd> + click) and add them all to the Shelf in one drag.

### 6. Move keepers to the destination

Once you've gone through all the photos:

1. Navigate to your `Keepers` folder (or create one with <kbd>Ctrl</kbd> + <kbd>Shift</kbd> + <kbd>N</kbd>)
2. Hover the Shelf to expand it
3. Click **Move here**

All your picks land in the `Keepers` folder in one operation. Press <kbd>Ctrl</kbd> + <kbd>Z</kbd> to undo if needed.

---

## Faster variant: keyboard-only flow

1. Press <kbd>Ctrl</kbd> + <kbd>4</kbd> (Icon view) and select the first photo
2. Press <kbd>Space</kbd> (Quick Look)
3. <kbd>→</kbd> to advance, <kbd>Esc</kbd> + drag-to-shelf for keepers
4. Repeat until done

---

## Tips

- **Split View helps** — Open the destination `Keepers` folder in the right pane (<kbd>Ctrl</kbd> + <kbd>Shift</kbd> + <kbd>E</kbd>). When you find a keeper, drag it from the Shelf directly into the right pane instead of navigating away.
- **Quick Look stays in sync** — If you delete a photo (<kbd>Delete</kbd>) while Quick Look is open, it automatically advances to the next one.
- **RAW files** — SPAN Finder uses the Windows imaging pipeline, which supports RAW formats if you have the manufacturer's codec installed (Canon CR2, Nikon NEF, Sony ARW, etc.).
